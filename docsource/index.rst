COVID-19 in England
===================
The aim of this project is to predict daily evolution of COVID-19 pandemic in the UK to help policy makers and create a public discourse about transparent science at the time of crisis, based on cutting edge epidemic models calibrated on daily number of infected and deaths (age-structured).
